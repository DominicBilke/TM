ThinMusic Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us, and it is our policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect while operating this website. This policy outlined here should help you understand how we collect and make use of personal information.

  • We will only collect information that is strictly neccessary for the operation of this website.
  • We will only retain personal information as long as necessary for the fulfillment of this website's purpose.
  • We will not share your information with any third party outside of our organization, other than as necessary to fulfill the website's purpose (e.g. play music) or your request (e.g. scrobble a track).
  • We will collect personal information by lawful and fair means with the knowledge or consent of the individual concerned.
  • We will protect personal information by reasonable security safeguards against loss or theft, as well as unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use or modification.
  • We are committed to conducting our business in accordance with these principles in order to ensure that the confidentiality of personal information is protected and maintained.
  • We may change our Privacy Policy from time to time, at our sole discretion.

Should you have any questions about how your personal data is handled, please email support@thinmusic.com and we will be happy to respond.
